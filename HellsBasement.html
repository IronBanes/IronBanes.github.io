Overview

Hell's Basement is a bot made by Ironbanes.
This bot's main feature is a single minigame. This minigame consists of the host (aka the bot) sending a random player to a text channel called #hell. When there, the player must flip a coin. If it lands on heads, the player loses. If it lands on tails, then the game will go on. The player can pick another person to be sent down to hell's basement who then must flip a coin. This process repeats until you get everyone who is not afk into Hell's Basement. Then you win!
